Arrow Board

How many times have you passed a highway construction project, safely guided by a flashing traffic "arrow board"? Now you can have your very own arrow board, in the palm of your hand.

The Arrow Board app features all standard operating modes as specified by the US Department of Transportation. It also features selectable speed, multiple color schemes, and the option to specify your own colors if you choose.

Available for free for all Apple devices (iOS 14 or later).
